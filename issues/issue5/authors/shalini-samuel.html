Shalini Samuel, author of Singing Soul comes from India's southern tip. She started her writing journey as a blogger. Poetry was her unfulfilled dream then. She explored poetry and slowly started learning the nuances of it. Apart from writing she also works as freelance editor. Her poems have been published in various online and print magazines and anthologies. She has edited few novels. Her second poetry collection The Painted Life is scheduled to be published within this year. Now she is writing her third poetry collection.
