Robin Lucas is a writer and humorist, and an infrequent poet… but a frequent #HashtagWhore on Twitter. He began writing at 22, mostly by accident, for the same reasons many writers began writing by accident: 1) he had a typewriter, 2) he had plenty of time thanks to being underemployed, and 3) he had a sudden urge to express his festering self-indulgence (third-person bios, for example). He continues to write for similar reasons, except #1 because his typewriter was eventually stolen—probably by an irritated roommate tired of hearing the keys tapping at all hours of the night. This is Robin's second appearance in In-flight Literary Magazine. He blogs at dryhumpingparnassus.com.
