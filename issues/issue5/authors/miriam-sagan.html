Miriam Sagan blogs at Miriam's Well. She is the author of 25 books, including the recent collection, SEVEN PLACES IN AMERICA: A Poetic Sojourn. Recently won New Mexico Literary Arts Gratitude Award in Poetry, and has received the Santa Fe Mayor's Award for Excellence in the Arts. Sagan also does text and grassroots installations--most recently at Salem Art Works and at The Betsy Hotel on Miami Beach.

Her second novel BLACK RAINBOW is forthcoming this fall from Sherman Asher Books.
