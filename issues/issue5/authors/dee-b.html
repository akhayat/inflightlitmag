Dee B. was raised on a healthy diet of crippling food allergies, missing pets, cabin trips, and punk ideologies. He received a B.A. in English and a minor in philosophy at Indiana University of Pennsylvania and still misses frequenting the town’s local video rental store. His work has appeared in Black & Grey magazine. Dee B. enjoys spending most evenings archiving hilarious quotes he’s heard from friends and strangers into his phone for bringing up in future conversations.
