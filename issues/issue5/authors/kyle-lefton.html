Kyle Lefton, 23, Los Angeles, CA. 

There are many different influences and styles inspiring Kyle's work. Musing on the timbre and free-flow of jazz, to the crushing maelstrom of lo-fi black metal, music of all genres continue to shape the language of his poetry. Kyle infiltrated the Sacramento poetry scene in 2013 and gained a following among the spoken word poets of Northern California for nearly two years. As an emerging professional in the environmental compliance industry, Kyle is deeply concerned with the nature of relationships between people, their environment, and the mysteries surrounding both. Themes within his work revolve around the quest for understanding, the yearning for spiritual connections, and a withstanding hope for the individual within an otherwise doomed species. The pieces selected for Paper Plane Pilots are rare, in the sense that they are extremely personal and directly address familial relationships and conflict.
