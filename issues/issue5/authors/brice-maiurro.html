Brice Maiurro was born and raised in Denver, Colorado. When not writing poetry and short fiction, he spends his time exploring the Rocky Mountains and drinking excessively in the city. His poems can be seen on The Denver Poetry Map and on Word Riot. He is currently working on completing a great American novel. It may be a while before he finishes this. In the mean time, you can read his poems on his blog, flashlightcityblues.com.
