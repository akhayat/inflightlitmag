Do the same thing
every day. Try
to stop suffering.
Add solar powered
blinking lights
shaped like dragonflies
to an old stump and three
little piñon trees.
Act like an actor.
Cook dirty rice.
Drink two fingers of vodka.
Decide to kill myself
rather than work
at Walmart.
Lose weight. Remember
being fat. All the world’s
a stage.
Knit a piece in the shape
of a sonnet.
Think about which dead people
would like
my new blinking lights.
Do not kiss
with a mouthful
of oatmeal.
Wonder—what is a wife?
Think about famous minimalists
who killed their wives.
Are there women minimalists?
Draw a line.
It is neither here
nor there. Claim
to have invented something.
Criticize other people’s
raising of children.
Tell them to try
and lighten up
a bit
and be greeted with
incredulity.
Never give up
cigarettes completely.
Realize you haven’t
worn pantyhose
in over twenty years
and yet
you have a job.
Be surprised. Your feet
don’t really care
about you. Yet they will not
fail you now.
