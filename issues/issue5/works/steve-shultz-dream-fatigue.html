Why do you want to be
something other than?
How about
trying to be
completely more than.

I went to bed sober,
but I still found myself
sticky at the bar.
Looking for you.
Adding them up.
Knocking them back.
Looking for you.

Why do you want to be
something you are not?
How about
trying to be
not so completely useless.

More do, less try.
But I still find myself
in your dreams,
sticky sheets.
Looking for me.
Adding them up:
the reasons I am less than
one.
