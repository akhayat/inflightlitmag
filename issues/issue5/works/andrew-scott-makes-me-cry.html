I am not an emotional man.
There are motions
that expectantly make me cry.

The feeling overcomes so subtly
that this stoic face
fights away the small tears.

So many causes to it.
Seems as age creeps in
one cannot help
but feel sentiment
and think of legacy
and influence.

So many memories and thoughts
that would never have a second glance
in the pages of yesteryear
cross my now present mind.

I am not an emotional man
however now it seems
the small things make me cry.
