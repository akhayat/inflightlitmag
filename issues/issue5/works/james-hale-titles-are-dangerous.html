Tonight is a sauna
Thanks to the steam engine of its tropics and its fever
As we're sweating into clam chowder
The atmosphere has become a sweat lodge with the feeling of spilled syrup
As the summer wears a headdress during a game of cowboys and Indians
As we try to cool down like shelved beer
Fans are airs, merry-go-round
Air conditioners try to create an ice age
Shade becomes precious real estate
Water becomes wine
Bedtime, clothing optional
Windows open, welcoming breeze
Soon all this will be,
But a memory
Until then I'll silk screen silhouettes in sweat on my sheets.
