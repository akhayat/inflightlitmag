I am
Glad. I am busy, I am in over my bed,
I am low spirited. I am 18 and stick-skinny
hunched in a habit not in low spirits. I remember
being enclosed by something more than you.

Wait, let’s talk about some real fears, she says:
-like what if I’m nothing, but questions, when everyone else is answered?
-or how either mermaids can’t cry under water, or they’re always crying—swimming
in nothing, but tears.
-What about if you put your heart into something,
or someone and lose your mind along with it,
does that mean they’re somehow connected?
-What if I forget,
what I’m like in real life?
Then to kissing you, like kissing lipstick and hugging coats. And never knowing,
that calling someone cute could be an insult—but it is, to a young girl, who is also glad
and not yet a woman.
