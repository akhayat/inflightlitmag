Forgive me, you’re the bee’s knees, he says.
Even if I were the grasshopper’s elbows, I would
never. Your blue crew neck makes me rue the day.
Why do you always wear it?

That prick is a sword now
no longer crossing hearts but eyes.

You were everything.

People say to look for signs. The signs are lies;
he lies as often as the sun rises. Goodbye. Goodbye.
