Brittany Piche was born in San Diego, California. She lived her life at the beach enjoying the soothing sounds of retired surfers and holistic healers. She shifted her atmosphere dramatically when she moved to Los Angeles 7 years ago to pursue her writing career. Today she lives out her days gallivanting through streets, stores, coffee shops, and bars in search of her next story. She is fascinated by the human condition, and goes to great lengths to understand the psychology of people that stand out in society.
