Matt Prater's work has been published by magazines from four continents, with poems recently appearing in Appalachian Heritage, drafthorse literary journal, and The Honest Ulsterman. A graduate of Radford and Appalachian State universities, he will begin work on an MFA at Virginia Tech in fall 2015. Currently a writing instructor at Emory & Henry College, he lives in Saltville, Virginia. Mono No Aware, his first chapbook, is forthcoming from Finishing Line Press.
