Ewan Cameron is a resident of Spokane, Washington, a studier and maker of language, and an avid lover of birds (though with great disdain for crows). Born in eastern Montana to a Scottish family, his investments primarily concern that of calligraphy, poems, and the Chinese fiddle – two of which have grown somewhat bearable to hear. His international experiences remain limited to that of Scotland, England, Holland, and Japan, though he should like to one day visit Italy, or Spain, or most anywhere – ideally, finding a peaceful hole there in which to spend the remainder of his days quite happily.
