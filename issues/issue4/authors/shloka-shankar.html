Shloka Shankar is a freelance writer from Bangalore, India. Her poems have recently appeared/forthcoming in Silver Birch Press, Otoliths, Jaggery, Literary Orphans, moongarlic e-zine, Inky Needles, The Gambler Mag, Yellow Chair Review, Straight Forward Poetry, and so on. She is also the founding editor of the literary & arts journal, Sonic Boom.
