Margrét Helgadóttir is a Norwegian-Icelandic writer and editor who lives in Copenhagen, Denmark. Her stories have appeared in various magazines, journals and print anthologies. Her debut book "The Stars Seem So Far Away" was published by Fox Spirit Books in February 2015. She's co-editor of an annual (fiction) coffee table book series: European Monsters (2014) and African Monsters (2015). She's also editor of the anthology Winter Tales in 2015.
