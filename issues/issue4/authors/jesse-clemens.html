Jesse Clemens lives in Los Angeles, CA, and is currently pursuing an M.A. in English/Creative Writing from California State University, Northridge. He was born and raised as an Angeleno, and tirelessly fights against the apathetic forces that think LA is lame. He is an INFJ on the Meyers-Briggs scale, so if he leaves right in the middle of an intense conversation to go huddle in a dark corner and read, please don't be alarmed; this is normal. He is rarely found not eating tacos.
