Lula Avila was born in Mexico, but has not stopped moving since she learned to walk, and has not stopped writing since she learned to write. She won the Special prize at the Bodegas Paternina short story contestes at La Rioja in 2012. She was published in the Antologia de micro relatos by Editorial Paroxismo in 2015. She currently resides in Brazil and blogs at www.lulaavila.com.
