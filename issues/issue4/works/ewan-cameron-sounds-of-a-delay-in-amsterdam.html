Whence, since whatever time, I sat close to the girl aside me. Beautiful, silken, boisterous, large and plentiful… her mother tongue. Russian bore no beauty – not without a proper maker. An old way perhaps, never worrying of the face or nature.

Translation, as a means of disillusion, was inevitable: Mr. Yamada and his tales of Rome.
