You told us
your life
was going to be
                          
a dead run
or a dead stop,

“and absolutely nothing else!”

Then you smashed
down the pedal
and held it there
                          
until all your
haughtiness
(and the highway)
was gone. Well,

you won't need  
cigarettes or gas
where you’ve gone:

cold sky above,
crashing sea below,

and absolutely nothing else.

Farewell, farewell

(idiot),

farewell.
