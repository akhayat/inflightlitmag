All has already been said and
done and seen and
shown and dreamed and
no lands left to explore and
enemies to defeat and
treats to eat that I haven't tasted before
in some other form.
All sins have been committed,
all crimes exposed to public indignation,
all words created, all forms of life
in every possible combination
mixed.
An endless repetition, a swirl, a matryoshka
of quotes wearing a swine pearl necklace,
and a huge round skirt
with a hem that comes undone,
that's the wheel of time.
A giant standing on a giant's shoulder:
this is the world.
