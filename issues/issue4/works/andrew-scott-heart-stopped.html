She is lying there afraid.
Cords and tubes in places
that she does not understand.
Feeding her to keep alive
since her heart stopped.

Confused and thinking.
Wiping away the tears
she is searching for a reason
that her body is not working
and the blood cannot flow.

The visions of her life
are playing in her head.
Was it the not taking care
and filling her body
of chemicals that
caused her forty year old body
to be acting old?

The guilt and the fear
are playing with her.
Were the signs there before
when she saw her parents
fall the same way?

She is thinking of missing
her own daughter and granddaughter
grow and make memories together.
She does not want
to miss feeling that.

There is so much left to live
and to take in and experience
that she feels that she will not
as she lies there wondering,
wondering why her heart stopped.
