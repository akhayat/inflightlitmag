I didn't expect the comfort that came
When you stepped inside that house
The dust and flakes of unwanted character
Were quickly replaced by the lines of light
You had locked inside
Battlefields that only spit defeat
Were soaked with change
And dandelions grew from the cracks
Where bodies had eternally rested
We built a dresser and some chairs
Hung a mirror
Combined our spaces
The lines of light becoming waves
Clearing the debris
Realizing that this was not a house
This was me
At peace
