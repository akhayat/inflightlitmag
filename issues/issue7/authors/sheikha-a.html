Sheikha A. hails from Pakistan and United Arab Emirates and is the author of a short poetry collection titled Spaced (Hammer and Anvil Books, 2013) available on kindle. Her work appears in over 60 literary zines/journals/magazines such as Red Fez, Ygdrasil, Mad Swirl, A New Ulster, Pyrokinection, ken*again to name a few, and several anthologies by Silver Birch Press and Kind of a Hurricane Press. She edits poetry for eFiction India. More about her can be accessed on her blog.
