Witty Fay is a translator by trade who has published in the virtual world at www.destinypoets.co.uk, www.writerscafe.org, www.poetrysoup.com, www.indianavoicejournal.com, www.screechowl.com, www.inksweatandtears.co.uk, www.everywritersresource.com, www.versewrights.com and www.thegalwayreview.com - and on paper: a bilingual volume of poetry, Nefelibata (Brian Brixon Books, 2014). Wearing the many hats of the aspiring writer, she aims at unraveling prose and draws inspiration from the people she meets, the places she travels, and the books she reads. She lives in Europe with her family.
