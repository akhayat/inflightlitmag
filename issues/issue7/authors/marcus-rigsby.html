At his childhood bedside, Marcus was haunted by visions of shadow beings and malevolent talking animals. Today he writes stories about the parallel worlds where mythologies and children's nightmares come from. His short fiction has earned College of the Canyons' Associated Student Writing Award and two Dr. Michael McMahan Awards. His work is featured in Cul-de-Sac Magazine (2011 and 2012), the Northridge Review (Fall 2015), and The Mochila Review (2016). He would like to thank Mona Houghton, the great author, actor and teacher who provided (totally non-biographical) inspiration for "One Calling in the Desert."
