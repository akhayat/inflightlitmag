Gabor G Gyukics (b. 1958) Hungarian-American poet, literary translator is the author of 7 books of original poetry, 4  in Hungarian, 2  in English including A Hermit Has No Plural by Singing Bone Press in 2015, 1 in Bulgarian and 11 books of translations including A Transparent Lion, selected poetry of Attila József published by Green Integer in LA in 2006, and an anthology of North American Indigenous poets titled Medvefelhő a város felett published by Scolar in Hungarian in 2015 . He writes his poems in English (which is his second language) and Hungarian. He had lived in Holland for two years before moving to the United States where he'd lived between 1988-2002. At present he resides in the isle of Csepel in Budapest, Hungary.
