sat          away

from jejune desires of others’ permissions.  
a newness, this jum
-bled alphabet of contemporariness, this
language of self, affirmations.  an atypical
pivot.  a percentage of doubt.  a fraction
from my tongue wandered, searched.  a
permission from silence engaged my
intertwining thinking.          away           
I’m already hiding at 22.  tomorrow is the hope.  
but hope is a desirous configuration of
the mind’s dexterous mirages and
fantasies of textured indications.
