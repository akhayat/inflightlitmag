I lack
the ability or desire
to drown myself in anything
that equals loss
of control.  I am chronic
freak, clutching every wheel
with both hands.  My eyes
never blink.  My focus never waivers.
I am a stay-between-the-lines girl
in an lineless world.  I swallow nothing
but a nonsensical belief that this straight path
is what will save me
as I mindfully follow it through Hell.
