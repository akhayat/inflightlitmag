Yesterday I will resign in 
your escape through 
my city merging after 
the next, when tomorrow 
I was warm with 
the sun chucking militant
feet over the pale
cascade of 
strewn flowers 
from your impervious ease
on the backseat, 
as today I am arriving 
to the two empty glasses 
upon the piano, 
my fingers snake
between a stranger's thighs
and tapping foot 
flicks away the muck 
of your graphic cutouts, 
built into the fringe
of my brain,
sitting across from me still.
