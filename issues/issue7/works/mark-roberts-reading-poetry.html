4. reading and walking

a car jumps up in front of you
& lands on its back.
is that a polished sump
glistening
in the october sun
or a small fire
spreading across the fuel line?
you can tell by the bumper sticker
that the driver was not a poet
so you go looking
for a cafe that serves real coffee
& has a table outside
so you can continue reading.

5. deconstructing poetry

deconstructing poetry
in a building site

i long for power tools
buzz saws, angle grinders, hammer drills

rivet guns fixing images
to lines that don’t know
when to break.
