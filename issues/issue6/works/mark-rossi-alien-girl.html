Maybe I’m old fashioned but I don’t see this as science fiction.

I banged an alien broad ten minutes after she brought me aboard her spacecraft. Sadly the experience was disappointing. She didn’t know shit about curing cancer or why Jupiter is a galactic garbage disposal. But we agreed the demotion of Pluto was humanity at its dumbest.

I rode her or the spacecraft or whatever you wanna call it for a few hours until Earth started looking interesting again. Funny how you miss the things you loathe moments after you are separated from them. (That rule doesn’t work for In-Laws.)

The Alien girl was bored with her travels and could care less for planetary invasion. She only wanted a drink and a place to lay down her nappy neon head. I helped where I could; yet in the best tradition of earthmen I searched for an angle to make me rich or famous.
Naturally I couldn’t find a ray gun or an energy device or something super interstellar to dazzle scientists into handing over millions of tax dollars they stole from the public last week. Five billion light years and all I find is an alien girl tired of her alien life in search of some basic fun in the sun.

Maybe I’m old fashioned but I was expecting something more. Something alien.
