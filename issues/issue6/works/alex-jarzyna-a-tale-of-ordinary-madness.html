i rise
and float towards

the moon white
windows of the garden

where a man
lights a fire for the sausages

yellow flowers
test his love of butter

and the kids in crocs
build dams and bridges

butterflies
drink nectar through a straw

fluttering

their shadows
grow as they walk behind me

my brain starts
to fill up and overflow with black water

and the slow
flapping wings of rays

the fin of a great white
the tail of the humpback whale

as tiny fishes
fall from my mouth

bees crawl in
through the holes in my head

and i flop into bed

finally,

i'm awake
