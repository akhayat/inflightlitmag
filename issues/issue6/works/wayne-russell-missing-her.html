Back in the Summer of '98
when LSD trips had gone
array on moon lit strolls reciting
Nietzsche naked swing in
moonbeams with the blonde
goddess from Yugoslavia,
she couldn't sing karaoke in
English very well, much less
hold a tune.

We made love on her English
leather sofa, in her French
cul-de sac and I saw the heavens
descend upon us, if only for a little
while.

The Gulf of Mexico nights that we
spent within the quiver of Alabama
white sand beaches, shall resonate
within my soul forever.

I find myself missing her, sometimes.
