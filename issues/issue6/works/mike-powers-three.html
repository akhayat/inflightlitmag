i.


sex warmth, breathing pulsating
worm decay
like starshower spritzing your spectacular
chest,
my libido exceeds expectations
and we smell of primal
earth.



ii.

heart like crystalline, chrysalis cocooned
concave--
we'd rule the nights together armed with madness
old CDs and brotherhood,
the hum of your engine and my alcoholic
breath,
the stars run away with envious
abandon



iii.


all of nineteen,
albeit some maturing
ripening fruit,
candle wicks burnt till tips became as saturated
as we were.
eight a.m. and the girls run in,
it's time to gut pigs
in bloody, crispy fashion.



iv.

i remember how the fog rolled in and you held me
big arms around my teen chest,
captivated coat held us both
and the winter water gurgled,
a drowning symphony
penetrating vulgar dam wall
as the air left our bodies and formed
magical nebulas
above our heads.
