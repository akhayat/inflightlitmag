Nick Brouk  is a cynical optimist, with a deep seeded love of the melodic arts, stemming from an early exposure to classical literature. This eventually evolved into a long-term appreciation for classical era concertos and 1950's jazz orchestras. He likes to be seen as a neo-romanticist, who values individualism and intuition, while indulging himself on the most potent forms of emotion, coupled with a Greek-like emphasis on drama and the human figure.
