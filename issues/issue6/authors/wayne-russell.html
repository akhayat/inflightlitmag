World traveler, creative writer, people watcher, 
and consumer of fine grog, would be just a few 
ways that you could describe Wayne Russell.

Wayne was born and raised in Florida, and from
the age of four had a love of the written word 
and art. 

By early adulthood, Wayne decided to scrap his
childhood aspirations of being the next Schulz,
or Disney, and just let his pen and paper do the 
talking instead.
