Life is a tightrope act
ever seeking the right balance
swaying upon one thin line
never going too far right
or too far left, centered

So how is it that with you
I lost my center and fell?
Crashed into a million pieces
one huge jigsaw puzzle
scattered across the world

Even now, as I re-assemble
the pieces don't fit right
I am too jagged or square
too rounded or obtuse
my center a giant void

As misshapen as I am
Will I ever be balanced again?
