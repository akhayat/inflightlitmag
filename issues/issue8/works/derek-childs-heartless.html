Who are you
that steals my heart
with but a glimmer? Your face,
fit only for a portrait
to be admired by all,
captivates, so enthrallingly,
my attention,
forever undivided
from this moment onwards.

Who would allow me to partake in viewing a woman
of unanalysable beauty?
To be harassed
by the knowledge
that the miles between us
cannot ever be erased.

Once introduced,
there should be the option
of meeting for the first time,
but this luxury is unaffordable
to men much like myself,
who must express affection softly,
and watch,
in dreadful agony,
as those we long for
are plucked like roses
by the hands of romantics,
who are destined to meet
these few beautiful women first.

You are a dream,
inescapable
and unforgettable;
an image of desirous perfection,
who does not deserve solitude or loneliness,
but a life blessed
by happy memories.
My only regret
is I am not the person fated
to bless upon you such an honour.

I am not worthy of being remembered,
and so, come a winter’s day,
when the virgin snow
drifts upon the soil,
a man, not I,
will wrap his arms around your neck
and whisper softly in your ear,
that he will keep you warm this night,
and all the evenings to come.
You will believe the words he says,
and forget these words of mine,
unable to acknowledge
if it were reality that brought us together,
or if it were a dream,
and even so,
forgetfulness
will claim each of these stanzas,
until my final breath
fades within me.

I’m a transparent replacement for what love ought to be.
It’s pointless
to articulate romantic longings,
when their occurrence
is not fated to transpire.
You are the dream
I want to keep on dreaming;
the air I want to breathe;
the kiss I want to share forever;
the love I want to make each night.
If you become aware of this,
none of the fleeting chances
given as luck to all men,
would be bestowed upon my heart,
for emptiness
is the eternal passage
I have been set to walk.
