I halfway learned to fly an airplane:
20 hours of instruction.
I had never seen Catalina Island,
Disneyland,
Big Bear Lake
spreading below me.
You'll never love me big like the earth and sky.
You'll never love me thin and quick like a few thousand feet of air.
Your ears don't pop with our conversations.
I don't blind you like flying west in the afternoon.
I crashed my budget,
'cause the first time I stalled an airplane,
the controls went floppy
as we leaned way back at 3500 feet.
I saw the sky tipping over
and forgot you for an instant.
