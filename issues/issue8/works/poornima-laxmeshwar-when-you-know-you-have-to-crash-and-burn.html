You just came into my life to have fun.
You 24 and I 33.
You weren’t looking for me.
We just happened to each other.
For me in a moment my soul got sealed with yours.
It wasn’t your mistake.
I just didn’t know how to undo that.
So I divided myself into 2 halves.
One was for you - loving, adventurous, wild and free.
And the other half - insecure, scared of scars and still alone.
I didn’t know how to put the feeling of solitude in love to words.
I failed myself.
I couldn’t rise above the sea.
I could neither belong to the flow nor settle on the shore.
Allow me to die this slow chasing death. For love is about perishing and the afterlife.
Allow me to cry, be hurt, fall and even choke. I deserve every inch of this pain.
Allow me to become every bit of you before I lose the identity of who I am.
