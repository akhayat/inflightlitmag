1

walking my dog at night
we pass a banner
for the beecroft wombats
football team
my dog
stops and sniffs
for a minute
before pissing
on the plastic sign

2

in the early morning the golf club
turn on their sprinklers
a hissing drifts through
bedroom windows
the water smells sweet and illegal

there is a motorway behind
the 16th tee
occasionally you'll hear
a smash as a driver dozes off
dreaming of an infinite loop



3

we've been driving this motorway for days
the etag double pings an empty sound
every 15 minutes but we keep driving
looking for an exit

at the service centre they are giving
away food and petrol we learn to order
our meals by colour last time the blue
this time the grey next time maybe
the aquamarine

4

somewhere
behind the warehouse
the wombats are playing
their final game
and a dog
pisses
on a goal post
