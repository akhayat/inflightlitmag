I have trails of time
following me
like lost pups
whining for attention.
Women from my past
reach out
with splayed fingers
showing the rings
they received from someone else.
Lost friends,
acquaintances, co-workers
chatter at me
blah, blah, blah,
hey, hey, hey.
Triumphs and failures
blink red and green
in my rearview
tapping out an archaic code.
I walk on
moving through a soup
of decisions,
a miasma of
problems, headaches
and trials,
carrying all of my
friends and enemies along
for the ride.
