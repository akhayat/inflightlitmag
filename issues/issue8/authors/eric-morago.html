Eric Morago is a Pushcart Prize nominated poet who believes performance carries as much weight on the page as it does off.  He is the author of What We Ache For (Moon Tide Press, 2010) and currently serves as an associate editor for the online journal, FreezeRay Poetry.  His next full-length collection of poetry, published by Paper Plane Pilots, is due out in November 2016. Eric has an M.F.A in Creative Writing from California State University, Long Beach, and lives in Los Angeles.
