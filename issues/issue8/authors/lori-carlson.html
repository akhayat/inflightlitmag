Lori Carlson is a poet and short fiction writer. Writing has always been her passion and in her pursuit of that passion, she earned a BA in English and a Masters in Arts and Liberal Studies at Hollins University in Roanoke, VA. Ms. Carlson has published poems in GZINE the Magazine,Diverse Voices Quarterly and Calliope Magazine in recent years. She enjoys collecting books of poetry from seasoned poets and up and coming poets. She considers the Confessional Poets Anne Sexton and Robert Lowell as her greatest influences. When she isn't writing, Ms Carlson reads a wide genre of fiction including science fiction, horror and crime/mysteries. She also indulges in her favorite science fiction television shows which include Babylon 5 and all of the Star Trek series. And when she is not writing, reading or watching her favorite shows, Ms Carlson daydreams about Andromeda. Currently, she lives with her elderly father in VA, surrounded by the beautiful Alleghany Mountains. More of her work can be found at https://ionanerissa.wordpress.com and https://promptlywritten.wordpress.com
