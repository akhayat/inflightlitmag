Sometimes referred to as The Little Firecracker, Kristine Brown wanders about in search of what she likes, while turning away from unappetizing things like buffet restaurants that look pretty on the outside. She continues to work on her poetry, primarily through "The Five Hundred Cats Project" featured on her blog, Crumpled Paper Cranes. When not at her day job, Kristine paints, works on her prose, and dabbles in freelance editing. Some of her writing has been featured in Journal of Asian Politics and History, Sanglap: Journal of Literary and Cultural Inquiry, and Thought Catalog.
