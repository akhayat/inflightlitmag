Katrina Byrd, a Jackson, MS native and graduate of Millsaps College, is currently a student in the Mississippi University for Women’s Low Residency MFA in Creative Writing Program. Katrina, a Mississippi Arts Commission Artist Minigrant Recipient, is a writer and playwright. Several of her short stories have appeared in Black Magnolias’ Literary Magazine and several of her short plays have been performed locally as a part of the Fondren Theatre Workshop and Vicksburg Theatre Guild’s Ten Minute Play Projects.
