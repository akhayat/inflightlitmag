Derek Childs

Derek Childs is a published poet and editor, born and raised in Melbourne, Australia. For those who wish to ask, no, he has never seen a kangaroo, he has never climbed Uluru, and he has never visited the Sydney Opera House. Derek is at present undertaking post-graduate studies to become a secondary school teacher, though has previously completed courses in creative writing. In his spare time, he is a video gaming geek, an avid reader, an anime addict, an occasional cook, an aspiring connoisseur of alcoholic beverages, an uncontrollable procrastinator, a writer with delusions of grandeur, and a believer in true love. If you wish, you may check out his blog: http://totalovrdose.wordpress.com/ which is often best avoided, unless you have already had a nice glass of red.
