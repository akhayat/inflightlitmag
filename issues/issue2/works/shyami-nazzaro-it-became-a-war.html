It became a war
Between my love
And your fear.

Both fought for dominance;
Sometimes one was winning,
And sometimes the other.

And after all the skirmishes,
The shows of force,
I am afraid to be on the losing side,

Submitting to the nuclear winter,
In the aftermath of love and war,

Alone in the barrenness of scorched earth,
While you, thousands of miles away, declare Victory,
Because I was never able to conquer your heart.
