He was sitting in his office and looking noncommittally at the grille through his half closed door; no, perhaps it was half opened.
He was not sure; he was thinking differently.
For sure he was not focused but he counted ten grilles till three girls passed and disrupted his personal mathematical activity.
'I missed the last one.'he said to himself.
'Missing something and missing someone are my destiny now.'he thought for a moment.
'But how can I be responsible for someone else's idiosyncrasies.'another thought pacifies him a bit.
A girl who changes her profile pictures in every fifteen minutes cannot guarantee any relationship—either traditional or modern. Yes, they were in a trendy live-in relationship for the past two months, until one fine day Kamya announced that 'I want to move out, I quit.'
There were no questions asked, and consequently no one had to listen to stupid answers. 'God is gracious,'he thought. At least this time she couldn't irritate him with her long monotonous quasi-philosophical speeches.
Kamya used to say, Rajat pondered, 'I am like a river–untamed and intractable; build big dams to capture me.'
When he was busy in releasing himself, he only wanted to float in rather than erecting anything non-significant.
Time flew very fast when she was with him. Every night was a party, and each day a festival. As every vacation ends, his Saturdays and Sundays — Kamya's love and their live-in relationship ended abruptly. Now her new lover like Monday was mocking at him, saying — 'Work hard you louse!'
Anyway, he was single again, and desperately waiting for a new companion. Rajat's chain of thought broke when he heard a knock on his half-open, half-closed door. A long blonde girl, not beautiful but reasonably pretty, peeped inside his office with a request 'May I come in?'
Her accent was weird. But he was also not interested in her brain. Rather, her body would appease him.
'Yes, how could I help you?'he said.
'Actually, hmm…'she stammered a little, and then said 'I am Kamya's friend Soniya.'
'Look,'he interrupted the new girl, but he was re-interrupted by Soniya.
'I am single nowadays, and interested in you.'she said quite explicitly.
For the first time since their separation, he thanked Kamya for her generosity, and welcomed the new guest with a broad smile.
