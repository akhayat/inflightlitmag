MD Marcus is a freelance writer and poet living in the past. She equally enjoys bumping Dolly Parton and Cash Money Millionaires while riding around in her 1999 Toyota Avalon. Her articles and essays can be found in Simply Elevate Magazine, PBSNet, and Salon. Her poetry has been featured in the Red Dashboard Publishing anthology, "dis-or-der," as well as in Calliope Magazine. Please read everything she writes and go to her website mdmarcus.com.
