David Mucklow is a poet from the small mountain ski and ranch town of Steamboat Springs, Colorado. His poetry has been published in Wrist Magazine, and Crosscurrents, as well as a short story published online by Creative Colloquy. He is currently seeking acceptance into an MFA program for Poetry in the Fall. He is passionate about being outside, snowboarding, fly fishing, good beer and coffee, and currently works in lift operations at Steamboat Resort.
