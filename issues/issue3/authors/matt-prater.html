Matt Prater is a poet and writer from Saltville, VA. A writing instructor at Emory and Henry College in Emory, VA, his work has appeared in Appalachian Heritage, Floyd County Moonshine, The Hollins Critic, James Dickey Review, Motif, Now & Then: The Appalachian Magazine, and Still: The Journal, among other publications. His first chapbook, Mono No Aware, is forthcoming from Finishing Line Press (Georgetown, KY).
