Saadia Faruqi is a Pakistani American writer of both fiction and nonfiction works, focusing on the Muslim/Pakistani/immigrant experience in her writings. She is editor-in-chief of Blue Minaret, a new literary journal, and editor of the Interfaith Houston blog. Her fiction is forthcoming in Catch & Release, On The Rush, and Belle Reve Literary Journal, among others. Her short story collection "Brick Walls: Tales of Hope & Courage from Pakistan" will be published in June. She tweets from @saadiafaruqi.
