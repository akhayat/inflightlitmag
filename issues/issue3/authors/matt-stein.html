Matt Stein has been writing poetry for the past five years. Growing up with a science background, he didn't really dabble in the arts until he read Charles Bukowski's "What Matters Most is How Well You Walk Through the Fire". After he finished reading it, he immediately began to write his own poetry. He has now gone on to write over five hundred poems. Attending UBC for Electrical Engineering he learned lots of math and physics but now writes full time. If you like his poetry style be sure to check out his blog' or his free e-book 'Hold on Guys, but what if..." available on Kobo Books.
