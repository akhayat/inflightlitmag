The sound of day cancelling itself -
it begins as a howl and disperses
in a chorus of whispers.
In the forest of dreams,
new ideas are taking root.
Life, for us, has been a sequence
of moot points joined together
with the most tenuous thread,
wed to a backdrop
blossoming constantly.
That startling contrast
that counteracts the feeling
of boredom. So we went reeling
along the shores blistered with shells,
discussing the prospect of Hell,
and whether or not we fit
into its schemes.
This we deemed unlikely,
something not worth considering.
We wandered back
into the girth of the city,
sharing cigarettes,
kisses, words of regret.
The streets had bled out.
And that was okay -
in our own way, we were listening.
