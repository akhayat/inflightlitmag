In the hands of evil her innocence taken
Stripped of dignity
Physically beaten and left for dead 
Soul slowly fading 
Spirit protecting the gift in her growing  
Exterior healing would take time
The scar remains
Where once a light would shine
Finding the strength to nourish her womb
Line of assault always visible to her view

Standing alone in judgment was a daily occurrence
Interior pain ran deep in her heart
Mark of that night a reminder to shield
Never letting the fear become her identity
Embracing the miracle coming for her serenity

Concealed blessing ready to enter the world
Her willingness to give unconditional love
Never letting the moment define his value to her
Allowing him to grow unaware of what happened that night
Died loving her precious gift, alone doing what she thought was right
