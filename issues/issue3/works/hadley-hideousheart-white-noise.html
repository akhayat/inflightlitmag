Don't call me technophobe.
I've just got a mental postcard
from an analog world
where a song still triggered a shiver
and knowledge was earned by fatigue,
then lost in the underflow.
Everyone wanted to be someone
just like today.
Most of the time they couldn't,
but at least they could choose
to be no one.
It was still possible
and fully acceptable
to shun all human interaction
just for a day.
