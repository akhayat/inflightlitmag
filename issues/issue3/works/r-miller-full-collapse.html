As if by magic, the puzzle
abandons its completed state,
dispersing in a plague of locusts.
Just when I managed to focus

my complaining into a soapbox.
I'd been straining too hard, sure,
but straining is worthwhile
when you subtract the serpent

from the beginning.
The festival collapses on the revelers
in their devilish attire. Shots are fired,
sent to the bread lines

to be wined and dined
by overzealous bureaucrats
in Hallmark suits and this -
this is when the looters arrive

armed to the teeth with their
peculiar brand of solipsism.
I have to take a stand. Funny -
I tried to take a sit, but I missed the chair,

so I didn't fare so well
in this endeavor. I must now endeavor
more endeavors. In hors d'oeuvres,
in orders and acupuncture,

in dysfunctional dismantling.
I make a run for the Panhandle
brandishing my starched collar
and colorful vocabulary,

a daring do-gooder
with one foot in the grave,
and the other unsure of where to go next.
Subtext of a sexual nature

is stirring the pot.
I've got eight words left,
and no arrangement in mind.
A thick mask of smoke and whatever's behind.
