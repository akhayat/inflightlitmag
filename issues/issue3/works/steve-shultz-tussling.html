She speaks to me
via tears
sighs and slammed
doors go up
where there should
be windows
face worth
a thousand words
but of late we
speak very few
just because we know
each other’s minds
does not mean
we need not speak
tears run her
mascara though
she knows she
doesn’t need it
I prefer the headaches
from slammed doors
over the quiet as
I stare out the
window at the wind
tussling her hair
