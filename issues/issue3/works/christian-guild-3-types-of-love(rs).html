I. the has-been:
The first one. The one you wrote
poems about, dreading every last word later
when the has-been broke your heart multiple
times.
She leads you on one day,
Letting you go the next.

Strike one.

II. true (well, your significant other) love:
The forever one. The one you chose to have two kids
with.
The one you were heart-cautious
with.
“Please don’t hurt me!” you exclaim. (thank
You has-been. I really appreciate your hostility...)
The lover you never get bored
with.

The lover
who loves you
more you
than deserve.

III. The Never-Will-Be:
Oh, the fun one. The one who should’ve had your
heart.
The one who needs you the most.

She’s the young rebel. The one who needs
you to make her smile.

The one you have no sexual desire
for.
Just love.

Every one of your kin
dislikes her. However,
you can’t get over the
tingling of your skin
when she’s near.

She marries and has a hard life.
A hard life because you had the true love spoken of
In dreams.

All she dreamt of was you and

The Never-Will-Be.
