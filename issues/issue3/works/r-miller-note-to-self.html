Don't go where your hands
can't go. All together, we form
a row of deception, stepping
across accidental continents,

contemplating the wisdom
of the Mayan Calendar.
Our crisscrossing of the ritual
has accomplished nothing

but an eruption of fuckups.
Corruption of the brain locus,
an unfocused harvest starved
the newly aboriginal of original skin.

That's what the within without
shouted into my door.
Nothing more but sheepskin stretched
from here to the mirror,

mountains of glass
drizzled with horrible heat
and appetites for virginity. So what
if your teeth won't chew the river?
