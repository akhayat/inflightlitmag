Submit to Us

For poetry, please submit 3-5 poems for consideration. Submissions should be attached all in a single file in the form of a Word, Open Office, or Google document or a PDF. To get a feel for our style, feel free to roam around our literary magazine or our literary blog For fiction, our word count limit has no minimum (feel free to run with that) and the maximum is 2,500 words. For screenplays, yes, you totally heard us right.

Simultaneous submissions are totally fine with us but please PLEASE please notify us as soon as humanly possible if it is published elsewhere. Previously published work is okay, just let us know where so we can give proper credit where it’s due.

Unfortunately, we cannot offer any form of payment at this time. We allow one submission per issue per person.
