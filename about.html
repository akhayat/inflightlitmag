About Us

In-flight Literary Magazine is a quarterly magazine organized by Paper Plane Pilot Publishing. It is an addendum to their literary blog where they frequently post new original poetry, fiction, and visual art.

This literary magazine is open to the public for submissions with one featured Paper Plane Pilot captaining each issue.
