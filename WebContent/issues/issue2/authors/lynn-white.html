Lynn White lives in North Wales. Her work is influenced by issues of social justice, events she has witnessed, and people she has known or imagined. Her poem 'A Rose For Gaza' was shortlisted for the Theatre Cloud War Poetry for Today competition and has since been published. Poems have also recently been included, or are forthcoming, in Harbinger Asylum's A Moment To Live By anthology, the One Love Foundation's Love Anthology, Anomalie, Sonic Boom and Phizzog among others. The poem 'God Save the Sheep' was inspired by a local controversy. Sadly, the sheep prevailed!
