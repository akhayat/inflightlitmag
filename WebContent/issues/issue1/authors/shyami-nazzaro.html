Shyami Nazzaro was born in Maine and grew up in New York State, and now lives with her husband and two children in Dublin, Ireland. She is an unpublished novelist and constant poet; words are her first love. Her other interests including singing, playing guitar, violin, and flute, cycling, running, cooking, and baking. She is passionate about whole, vegan foods, living a healthy lifestyle, and being kind to the earth and all living beings.
